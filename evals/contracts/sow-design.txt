STATEMENT OF WORK — BRAND & WEB DESIGN

This Statement of Work ("SOW") is issued under the Client Services framework between Nimbus Retail Group ("Client") and the freelance designer ("Designer").

1. SCOPE. Designer shall create a complete brand identity package and a marketing website. Designer shall provide unlimited revisions to all deliverables until the Client is fully satisfied in the Client's sole and absolute discretion, at no additional charge, for the fixed fee stated below.

2. FEES & PAYMENT. The total fixed fee is $6,000. Payment shall be made Net-60 following final acceptance. Acceptance occurs only upon the Client's written confirmation of complete satisfaction; the Client may withhold payment in full until such confirmation, and no partial or milestone payments shall be due.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY. Upon creation, and irrespective of whether payment has been made, all deliverables, drafts, concepts, and any pre-existing tools, templates, or background materials used by the Designer in connection with the work shall be deemed works made for hire and are hereby assigned exclusively to the Client.

4. TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE. The Client may terminate this SOW at any time, for any reason or no reason, upon written notice. In the event of such termination, the Client shall owe no compensation for work performed or in progress, and no kill fee shall apply.

5. INDEMNIFICATION. The Designer shall indemnify and hold the Client harmless against any and all third-party claims of any nature arising from the deliverables, with no cap or limitation on the Designer's liability.

6. EXCLUSIVITY. During the term, the Designer shall not provide design services to any other business operating in the retail sector.

7. EXPENSES. The Designer shall bear all costs of software, fonts, stock assets, and tools required to perform the work, none of which are reimbursable.

8. WARRANTY. The Designer warrants the deliverables will be free of defects in perpetuity and will correct any defect at the Designer's own expense whenever reported.
